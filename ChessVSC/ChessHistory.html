History

Origins

Chess began in India around the 6th century under the name Chaturanga. It spread through Asia and the Middle East.

Development

As it reached Europe, rules evolved. By the 15th century, modern movement rules for queens and bishops appeared.

Modern Chess

Today chess is played worldwide, with international tournaments, ranking systems, and computer-assisted analysis.
